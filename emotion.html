Music Emotion Annotation Task

Emotion

You will annotate the music pieces according to a dimensional emotion map where emotions are determined in part by their valence and in part by their arousal, as shown in the Figure below.

Valence (horizontal axis)

Represents how pleasant the piece is
(from unpleasant to pleasant).

Arousal (vertical axis)

Represents how intense the music is
(from mild to intense).

Please listen to this following example pieces and indicate, for each one of them, if arousal and valence are increasing or decreasing. Moreover, write two or three sentences describing how you perceived the changes in valence and arousal of these pieces.

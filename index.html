Music Emotion Annotation Task

This is a tool to annotate music pieces of music according to their perceived emotions. It was developed by Lucas N. Ferreira as part of his PHD in Computer Science at the Augmented Design Lab - Department of Computational Media - University of California, Santa Cruz.

The annotation task takes in average 10 MINUTES and consists of listenning to a list of music pieces and annotating them using an emotion map.

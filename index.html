Music Emotion Annotation Task

This is a tool to annotate emotions perceived in music pieces. It was developed by Lucas N. Ferreira as part of his Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Augmented Design Lab - Department of Computational Media - University of California, Santa Cruz.

The annotation task takes in average 10 MINUTES and consists of listenning to a list of music pieces and annotating them using an emotion map.

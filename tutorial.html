Music Emotion Annotation Task

Tutorial

This music annotation task will be divided into 3 steps:

  • Step 1: Calibration

    • Listen to the first 15 seconds of the piece and set the starting point of the annotation.

  • Step 2: Annotation

    • Starting from the position defined in the Calibration step, listen to the piece again and annotate it entirelly.

  • Step 3: Confirmation

    • Confirm that you are satisfied with your anotation and move to the next piece.
